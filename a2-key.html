A2 Key (KET) Preparation Course

Our A2 Key course is perfect for beginners looking to build a strong foundation in English. This course prepares you for the Cambridge A2 Key exam, also known as Key English Test (KET).

What You'll Learn

  • Essential vocabulary for everyday situations
  • Basic grammar structures
  • Reading comprehension skills
  • Writing simple texts and messages
  • Listening skills for understanding everyday English
  • Speaking practice for common situations

Benefits of This Course

  • Prepare with confidence at an official Cambridge Test Preparation Center
  • Learn from highly-qualified instructors with years of experience in Cambridge exam preparation
  • Build confidence in using English for everyday communication
  • Prepare effectively for the A2 Key (KET) exam
  • Small class sizes for personalized attention
  • Regular progress assessments
  • Access to additional learning resources

Course Structure

The course runs for 10 months, with one or two hour sessions per week. Each session includes a balanced mix of:

  • Interactive classroom instruction
  • Practical language exercises
  • Exam-style practice tests
  • Guided self-study activities

You'll receive personalized feedback throughout the course, ensuring you stay on track and feel supported every step of the way. Our experienced instructors use a communicative approach to ensure you gain practical language skills while preparing for the exam.

About the A2 Key (KET) Exam

The A2 Key exam assesses your ability to communicate in simple situations. It consists of three papers:

  • Reading and Writing (1 hour 10 minutes)
  • Listening (30 minutes)
  • Speaking (8-10 minutes)

Our course thoroughly prepares you for all these components, giving you the skills and confidence to succeed.
