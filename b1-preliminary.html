B1 Preliminary (PET) Preparation Course

Our B1 Preliminary course is designed for intermediate learners aiming to develop practical English skills for everyday use. This course prepares you for the Cambridge B1 Preliminary exam, also known as Preliminary English Test (PET).

What You'll Learn

  • Expanded vocabulary for various topics
  • Intermediate grammar and sentence structures
  • Reading comprehension and writing techniques
  • Listening skills for understanding native speakers
  • Speaking practice for real-life situations
  • Strategies for effective communication

Benefits of This Course

  • Prepare with confidence at an official Cambridge Test Preparation Center
  • Learn from highly-qualified instructors with years of experience in Cambridge exam preparation
  • Improve your ability to communicate in everyday English
  • Gain confidence in using English in work or study environments
  • Interactive classes with focus on practical language use
  • Regular mock exams to track your progress
  • Comprehensive preparation for the B1 Preliminary exam

Course Structure

The course runs for 10 months, with one or two hour sessions per week. Each session includes a balanced mix of:

  • Interactive classroom instruction
  • Practical language exercises
  • Exam-style practice tests
  • Guided self-study activities

You'll receive personalized feedback throughout the course, ensuring you stay on track and feel supported every step of the way. Our experienced instructors use a communicative approach to ensure you gain practical language skills while preparing for the exam.

About the B1 Preliminary (PET) Exam

The B1 Preliminary exam assesses your ability to communicate in English in practical, everyday situations. It consists of four papers:

  • Reading (45 minutes)
  • Writing (45 minutes)
  • Listening (30 minutes)
  • Speaking (12-17 minutes)

Our course thoroughly prepares you for all these components, giving you the skills and confidence to succeed.
