B1 Preliminary (PET) Preparation Course

Our B1 Preliminary course is designed for intermediate learners aiming to develop practical English skills for everyday use. This course prepares you for the Cambridge B1 Preliminary exam, also known as Preliminary English Test (PET).

What You'll Learn

  • Expanded vocabulary for various topics
  • Intermediate grammar and sentence structures
  • Reading comprehension and writing techniques
  • Listening skills for understanding native speakers
  • Speaking practice for real-life situations
  • Strategies for effective communication

Benefits of This Course

  • Improve your ability to communicate in everyday English
  • Gain confidence in using English in work or study environments
  • Interactive classes with focus on practical language use
  • Regular mock exams to track your progress
  • Comprehensive preparation for the B1 Preliminary exam

Course Structure

The course runs for 15 weeks, with two 2.5-hour sessions per week. Classes include a mix of teacher-led instruction, group activities, and individual practice.
