About the C2 Proficiency (CPE) Exam

The C2 Proficiency exam is the most advanced English language qualification. It proves you can communicate with the fluency and sophistication of a highly competent English speaker. The exam consists of four papers:

  • Reading and Use of English (1 hour 30 minutes)
  • Writing (1 hour 30 minutes)
  • Listening (40 minutes)
  • Speaking (16 minutes)

Our comprehensive course prepares you thoroughly for all components, ensuring you have the skills and confidence to excel at this highest level of English proficiency.
