B2 First (FCE) Preparation Course

Our B2 First course is tailored for upper-intermediate learners aiming to achieve a high level of English proficiency. This course prepares you for the Cambridge B2 First exam, formerly known as First Certificate in English (FCE).

What You'll Learn

  • Advanced vocabulary and idiomatic expressions
  • Complex grammar structures and language use
  • Advanced reading and writing strategies
  • Listening comprehension for various accents and contexts
  • Fluent and natural speaking skills
  • Exam techniques and time management skills

Benefits of This Course

  • Prepare with confidence at an official Cambridge Test Preparation Center
  • Learn from highly-qualified instructors with years of experience in Cambridge exam preparation
  • Achieve a level of English required for university entrance
  • Enhance your career prospects with internationally recognized certification
  • Develop skills for academic and professional English use
  • Personalized feedback and progress tracking
  • Access to extensive online resources and practice materials

Course Structure

The course runs for 20 weeks, with two 3-hour sessions per week. Each session includes a balanced mix of:

  • Intensive language practice
  • Mock exams and exam strategies
  • Personalized coaching and feedback
  • Interactive group activities

Our experienced instructors use a communicative approach to ensure you gain practical language skills while preparing for the exam.

About the B2 First (FCE) Exam

The B2 First exam assesses your ability to use English confidently in social and professional contexts. It consists of four papers:

  • Reading and Use of English (1 hour 15 minutes)
  • Writing (1 hour 20 minutes)
  • Listening (40 minutes)
  • Speaking (14 minutes)

Our course thoroughly prepares you for all these components, giving you the skills and confidence to succeed.
