OUR ENGLISH COURSES

Tailored English Courses for Every Level

From beginners to advanced learners, we offer comprehensive courses to help you achieve your English language goals

  • A2 Key (KET) Preparation

    Our A2 Key course is perfect for beginners looking to build a strong foundation in English. We focus on:

    • Essential vocabulary and grammar structures
    • Basic reading and writing skills
    • Everyday listening and speaking practice
    • Confidence-building exercises

    By the end of this course, you'll be able to communicate in simple, everyday situations.

    Elementary Level

  • B1 Preliminary (PET) Preparation

    Our B1 Preliminary course helps you develop practical English skills for everyday use. We emphasize:

    • Expanding vocabulary for various topics
    • Intermediate grammar and sentence structures
    • Reading comprehension and writing techniques
    • Listening skills and conversational practice

    This course will enable you to confidently navigate most everyday situations in English.

    Intermediate Level

  • B2 First (FCE) Preparation

    Our B2 First course is designed to take your English to an advanced level. We concentrate on:

    • Complex grammar and sophisticated vocabulary
    • Advanced reading and writing strategies
    • Listening comprehension for various accents
    • Fluent and natural speaking skills

    This course prepares you for academic study or professional use of English.

    Upper-Intermediate Level

  • C1 Advanced (CAE) Preparation

    Our C1 Advanced course is for those aiming for high-level English proficiency. We focus on:

    • Nuanced and idiomatic language use
    • Critical reading and analytical writing
    • Advanced listening and note-taking skills
    • Confident speaking on complex topics

    This course equips you with the skills to use English at a professional or academic level.

    Advanced Level

  • C2 Proficiency (CPE) Preparation

    Our C2 Proficiency course is for those seeking to master the English language. We emphasize:

    • Sophisticated and nuanced language use
    • Advanced literary and academic texts
    • High-level listening and speaking skills
    • Mastery of subtle language expressions

    This course helps you achieve near-native proficiency in English.

    Proficiency Level

  • Conversational English

    Our Conversational English course is perfect for those who want to improve their speaking skills without exam pressure. We focus on:

    • Practical, everyday conversation topics
    • Pronunciation and natural speech patterns
    • Idiomatic expressions and colloquialisms
    • Cultural aspects of English-speaking countries

    This course helps you gain confidence in real-world communication scenarios.

    All Levels Welcome
